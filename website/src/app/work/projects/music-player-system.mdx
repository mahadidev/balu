## Commands

### Basic Playback
- `!play <song/url>` - Play music from YouTube
- `!play <song/url> --related` - Play song and add related songs to queue
- `!pause` - Pause current song
- `!resume` - Resume playback
- `!skip` - Skip to next song
- `!stop` - Stop music and clear queue

### Queue Management
- `!queue` - Show current queue
- `!playlist <url>` - Load entire YouTube playlist
- `!volume <0-100>` - Adjust volume

### Advanced Controls
- `!repeat [off/track/queue]` - Set repeat mode
- `!shuffle` - Toggle shuffle mode
- `!join` - Join your voice channel
- `!leave` - Leave voice channel

### Slash Commands
- `/play` - Interactive play command with autocomplete
- `/play <song> --related` - Interactive play with smart related songs
